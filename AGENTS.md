# Agent Instructions

This repository is public-facing and claim-safe.

## Non-negotiable boundaries

- Do not claim that physical laws literally want, think, feel, or make conscious choices.
- Do not frame FCR as science proof, medical guidance, legal advice, spiritual authority, or prediction machinery.
- Do not weaken consent, evidence, or repair language in the name of flow.
- Do not add hidden network calls, accounts, telemetry, or backend services without a separate explicit design review.
- Keep the app local-first and transparent.
